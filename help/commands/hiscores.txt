**Hiscores** command help page.
Usage: **hiscores "<player>"** *[-ironman | -ultimate | -hardcore | -deadman | -seasonal] -full -virtual*
Aliases: **hiscores**, **hiscore**, **hs**

**"<player>"**
- The player's name, wrapped in quotation marks.

**[ -ironman | -ultimate | -hardcore | -deadman | -seasonal ]**
- The desired gamemode. Only select one. You may abbreviate it as well (i.e., -iron, -dmm, -s)

**-full**
- Displays the full hiscores, with rank, level, and experience for each skill. You can also use -f.

**-virtual**
- Displays virtual levels instead of actual levels. You can also use -v.
